# Valencia Itineraries — March 21-28, 2026

**Key constraints:**
- **Sat Mar 21** = Arrival at 20:00. Settle in, grab dinner nearby. No sightseeing.
- **Sun Mar 22** = FREE museum day (La Lonja, towers, IVAM, Ceramics, Almoina — save ~12 EUR/pp)
- **Mon Mar 23** = Many attractions CLOSED (Sagunto, Xativa, Requena, most museums). Good for: Bioparc, beach, cycling, Albufera
- **Sat Mar 28** = Must be at airport by 12:00. Morning only — quick coffee + last stop, then metro.
- Tourist Card 72h = activate on a day you use transport + paid museums heavily
- Paella = lunch dish only. Menu del dia = weekday lunchtimes only.

### Day 6 — Thursday Mar 26: DAY TRIP — pick one!
*Choose based on your mood after 5 days:*

**Option A: Sagunto** (history, easy, cheap) — Train 30 min. Free castle + Roman theatre + Jewish quarter. ~25 EUR/pp total. Back by 17:00.

**Option B: Xativa** (best castle, Borgia history) — Train 50 min. Spectacular double castle, upside-down king painting. ~40 EUR/pp total. Full day.

**Option C: Montanejos Hot Springs** (nature, swimming) — Only possible Thu! Bus at 10:00. Swim in 25°C thermal pools + gorge hike. ~35 EUR/pp total. Full day.

**Option D: Requena** (wine caves, medieval) — Train 75 min. Underground caves + wine country. ~40 EUR/pp total. Full day. Bring layers (cold!).

| Time | If Sagunto (half day) | If you're back early... |
|------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Evening | **Cathedral interior** (8 EUR) + climb Miguelete bell tower (+2 EUR) |
| 19:30 | Sunset from the bell tower |
| 21:00 | **Paella dinner** at Restaurante Levante (best in city, book ahead) — OK to break the "lunch only" rule once |

## WHICH DAY TRIP TO PICK?

| If you want... | Go to | Why |
|----------------|-------|-----|
| Best value (cheapest) | **Sagunto** | All free attractions. ~25 EUR/pp total. 30 min train. |
| Most impressive castle | **Xativa** | Double castle, Borgia history, upside-down king. Full day. |
| Nature + swimming | **Montanejos** | 25°C hot springs + gorge hike. Only possible Mon/Thu (bus). |
| Wine + underground caves | **Requena** | Medieval wine caves, silk museum. Full day. Cold! |
| Beach + Borgia palace | **Gandia** | Ducal Palace + fideua lunch + golden beach. 1h15 train. |
| Dramatic coast | **Peniscola** | Medieval fortress on the sea. GoT location. Long day (2.5h bus). |
| Mountain hiking | **Sierra Calderona** | Closest mountains. Castle ruins at 536 m. Check bus schedule! |

**Our top pick for one day trip:** Sagunto (morning) + Albufera sunset (afternoon) — combine them on the same day for the ultimate budget day out.

**If you do two day trips:** Sagunto + Xativa, or Sagunto + Montanejos (Thu).

## 72h TOURIST CARD — When to Activate

**Best activation day:** The day you first use transport heavily + visit paid attractions.

**Recommended: Activate Monday (Day 3)**
- Mon: Bioparc (covered) + metro/bus
- Tue: Oceanografic + Science Museum + Hemisferic (all covered) + metro
- Wed: Albufera bus #24 (covered)
- Card expires Wed evening. Thu onward: Valenbisi bikes + walking

This covers your 3 most expensive days. Don't waste it on Sat arrival (you land at 20:00).
